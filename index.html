Hey? What's the point of this?

This should be on the FAQ but I can imagine this might be too common of a question. I'm doing this for a few main reasons:

  1. I started on the concept for this before the balance discord launched it's website and icy veins started hosting ffxiv guides in ernest.
  2. I have friends who don't like the way a lot of the currently written guides are explained and I like helping my friends.
  3. It's fun! I enjoy FFXIV and I enjoy making silly little websites. That alone is enough to make me want to work on this. It's a passion project funded purely out of pocket and worked on purely out of love. (And maybe a little bit out of some residual spite of being tired of google doc guides...)
